Program

Overview of the academic program.

Lectures

The faculty in the summer school will cover a wide range of core topics related to learning and decision making in humans (and machines) such as reinforcement learning, learning from experience, social learning, multi-cue probabity learning etc.

Tutorials

In addition to the main lectures we will organize hands on tutorials about methods that you can use in your own research

Projects

What's more, there will be some additional time where the faculty will help students with their currect projects

Panel discussions

During the summer schoool we will host panels with leading figures in judgment and decision making research focused on topics such as career development, academic publishing and open science etc

Poster session

There will be two poster sessions where participants will present they research to other early career researchers and the faculty

Social activities

The summer school will be an opportunity to get together with other early career researchers from around the world. We are planning on having welcome On Saturday there will be a beach volley tournament and on Sunday you can join a hike on the nearby Catalonian mountains
