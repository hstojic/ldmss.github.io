How to apply?

Details about the application process.

Eligibility...

To apply for the summer school you should submit the following materials:

  • CV: An up to date CV focusing on your academic work, at most three pages long.
  • Motivation letter: A one-page letter detailing your motivation to participate in the summer school and what do you expect to gain.
  • Poster abstract: As a part of our program every participant will be required to present their work in a poster session. This does not have to be a polished work, it can be a report of a partial analysis of simulated or experimental data, or just an idea that you would like to feedback on. Broadly, it should be related to learning and decision making.
  • One reference letter: A reference letter sent to us on your behalf, detailing why you are a good candidate for the summer school. Ideally this letter should be from a person that is familiar with your academic achievements.

When applications open a link will be available here that will lead you to a form to submit all the materials.
